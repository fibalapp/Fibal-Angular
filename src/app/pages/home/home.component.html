The communities that inspired Fibal

Many people use Reddit and Facebook to ask personal finance questions. However, some people are afraid to share their information on Facebook because it is linked to their photos and friends. On the other hand, Reddit is too private and doesn't allow for building relationships.
Fibal offers a middle ground by letting you create a clean account with only the information you want to share to tell your full financial story without giving up your privacy.
